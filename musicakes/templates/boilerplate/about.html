About Us

Musicakes is a simple, experimental web application for artists to sell their music, and to tokenise the revenue streams from their music sold on this platform. Use this platform at your own risk!

In the future, we will be exploring other features such as artist/label tokens that can be sold in order to gain special privileges, such as access to exclusive content or the right to purchase limited edition merchandise (for those with a fervent fan base).

You can reach out to us at musicakes [dot] team [at] gmail [dot] com.
