Musicakes

Musicakes is a simple, experimental web application for artists to sell their music, and to tokenise the revenue streams from their music sold on this platform.
We enable the artist to determine how the payment should be split by representing shares to the proceeds as tokens (called Musicakes too). Each release will have 100 Musicakes tokens upon the creation of the smart contract. All 100 tokens will be sent to the artist's wallet address that was used at the time the release was created. Any user with the Musicake tokens for a release can claim a proportional amount of the payments made to that release's smart contract.
We may implement a 2% fee in the future to cover operating costs for AWS storage.

What can I do with Musicakes?

There are countless possibilities with what you can achieve with Musicakes.
If you are a label releasing a Various Artists compilation, you can equally split the revenue stream of that album by sending an equal amount of Musicakes to each contributing artist
If you co-produced an album with another artist, you can share the revenue stream of that album by transferring 50 out of 100 Musicakes to the other artist.
