Musicakes

Musicakes allows you to tokenise revenue from your music sales on this platform and distribute those tokens in whatever manner you desire.

Each token is called a Musicake, and 100 is created by default. 2 will be transferred to our platform as transaction fees (meaning we take a 2% fee), and the remaining 98 is transferred to your wallet address.
