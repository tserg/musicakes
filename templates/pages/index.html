Musicakes

Musicakes allows you to tokenise the revenue stream from your music sales on this platform and distribute those tokens in whatever manner you desire.

Each release will have its own token (called a Musicake). 100 Musicakes are created by default for each release. 2 Musicakes are transferred to a wallet address belonging to our platform as transaction fees (meaning we take a 2% fee), and the remaining 98 Musicakes are transferred to your wallet address.

What can I do with Musicakes?

There are countless possibilities with what you can achieve with Musicakes.

If you are a label releasing a Various Artists compilation, you can equally split the revenue stream of that album by sending an equal amount of Musicakes to each contributing artist

If you co-produced an album with another artist, you can share the revenue stream of that album by transferring 49 Musicakes to the other artist.
